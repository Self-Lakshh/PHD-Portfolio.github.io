Kutki Dahi Rice

Procedure

  • Allow the kutki to be cooked in a pan on a low flame
  • When it gets fluffy and mashable, take it out in a separate bowl
  • Add curd to the mixture and mix it well
  • In a separate pan, fry black cumin seeds and curry leaf and garnish it on top of dahi kutki

Ingredients

  • Kutki: 1 cup
  • Curd: 1 cup
  • Black cumin seeds: 1 cup
  • Curry leaves: 4-5 pcs
  • Oil: 2 tbsp.
  • Hot water: 1 glass
  • Salt to taste
