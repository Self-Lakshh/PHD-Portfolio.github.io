Ragi Ladoos

Procedure

  • Roast ragi flour in ghee till you get a nice aroma of ragi
  • Heat milk and add grated or powdered jaggery to it
  • Create a mixture of the cooked flour, khoya and cardamom
  • Make balls from the mixture while it is warm

Ingredients

  • Ragi flour: 2 cups
  • Milk: 1 cup
  • Jaggery to taste
  • Khoya: Roasted in ghee
  • Ghee: 1 cup
  • Grinded cardamom: 1 tbsp.
