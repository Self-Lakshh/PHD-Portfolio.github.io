Sanwa Cutlet

Procedure

  • Cook sanwa using hot water
  • Mix ground split chickpeas with curd, potato and make a paste
  • Heat oil; add cumin and black cumin seeds, fine chopped onion, ginger, garlic and green chillies to it
  • Add the cooked sanwa along with the paste to the mixture and mix it well. Add coriander and turmeric as well
  • When the mixture cools down, make a shape of it and fry

Ingredients

  • Sanwa flour: 1 cup
  • Water: 1 cup
  • Ground chana dal: 1 cup
  • Curd: 1 cup
  • Onion Garlic Ginger: 1 pcs
  • Green Chilli Paste: 1 tbsp
  • Boiled potatoes: 1 pcs
  • Oil: Half pan
  • Coriander powder: 1 tbsp.
  • Turmeric powder: ½ tbsp.
  • Cumin seeds: 1 tbsp.
  • Salt to taste
