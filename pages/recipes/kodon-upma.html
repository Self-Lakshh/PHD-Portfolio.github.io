Kodon Upma

Procedure

  • Wash kodon thrice, thoroughly
  • Cut onions, green chillies, and vegetables
  • Now heat oil in a non-stick pan. Then add mustard and cumin seeds in it. After a few seconds, add onion, green chilli, carrot, curry leaves and cook for 2 minutes on a medium flame
  • Add kodon, hot water and salt and cover it
  • Cook it thoroughly

Ingredients

  • Kodon flour: 1 cup
  • Black cumin seeds: 1 tbsp.
  • Curry leaves: 5-7
  • Hot water: 1 glass
  • Onions, green chillies and carrots (Fine chopped)
  • Salt to taste
