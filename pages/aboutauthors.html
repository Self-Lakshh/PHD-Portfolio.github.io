About Author

Dr. Vimla Dunkwal

Dean at College of Community Science, SKRAU, Bikaner (Rajasthan).

Done B. Sc. (Home Science) (1990) from Banasthali Vidyapith, Tonk, M. Sc. (Food & Nutrition) (1992) from Banasthali Vidyapith, Tonk, PG Diploma in Nutrition & Dietetics (1993) from Punjab University, Ph.D. (Food & Nutrition) (2006) from CCSHAU, Hisar, and LLB (2012) from MGSU, Bikaner. Currently pursuing MBA (Human Resource) from IGNOU. She has been engaged in academics and research for 25 years, with more than 12 years of experience as a professor. She specializes in the value addition of arid fruits and vegetables, millets, and ICT development to improve nutritional status. Her Ph.D. research was on “Development and nutritional evaluation of value-added products using oyster mushroom.” She has published more than 23 research papers in international journals and 24 in national journals, along with authoring 4 reference books and editing/contributing to 3 reference books. She holds two patents in the area of food technology and product development. She has been recognized with multiple awards, including appreciation awards for millet products, the National Nutrition Mission, “Eat Right Millet Mela”, the “Nutri Scholars Award” for “The Vegetarian Cake Treat”, the “Lifetime Achievement Award” for value addition of millets, and an award for a “Device for Preparing Multi Millets Cookies.” She is a life member of several professional societies such as the Nutrition Society of India and the Indian Dietetics Association, Jaipur, and established the agri-innovative incubation center “Marushakti,” registered under FSSAI.

Dr. (Er.) Parimita

Associate Professor at Department of Food Nutrition, College of Community Science, SKRAU, Bikaner (Rajasthan)

Done B. Sc. (Home Economics), M. Sc. (Adv Nut & Dietetics), B.Tech (food tech), M.Tech (Food Process Eng.), Ph.D. (Food Technology). She has been engaged in academics and research for eighteen years. Guided over thirty-five PG research works and guiding four PhD students. She has published more than thirty research papers in national and international journals; like Scopus and web of science. Also published three reference books based on own researches & edited two reference books written by foreign authors. She has three Indian patents published and one design patent. She is recognized peer reviewer in "Elsevier". She is peer reviewer/editorial board member in six NAAS rated/impact factor/various index listed international journals in the field agriculture, science & technology. She is awarded with four international awards in the field of food technology. She has served as UGC-HRDC and UPSC resource person.

Yashasvi Rathore

Research Scholar at College of Community Science, SKRAU, Bikaner (Rajasthan).

Completed B.Sc. (Home Science) (2020) from Swami Keshwanand Rajasthan Agricultural University, Bikaner, M.Sc. (Food & Nutrition) (2022) from Maharana Pratap University of Agriculture and Technology, Udaipur, and currently pursuing Ph.D. (Food & Nutrition) (2023 – Present) from SKRAU, Bikaner. She has been actively engaged in academics and research since her Master’s, with significant experience in clinical and therapeutic nutrition, public health, and community-based nutrition programs. Her research interests include diabetes education, gut health, lifestyle-related diseases, and evidence-based nutrition interventions. She has participated in programs such as the National Diabetes Educators Programme (NDEP) and the Gut Health Summit, gaining hands-on experience in dietary surveys, nutritional counseling, and community outreach. She has contributed to multiple research projects and aims to bridge academic research with practical impact in public health nutrition. She is committed to developing innovative, evidence-driven strategies to improve community well-being and collaborates actively with professionals and institutions focused on nutrition-driven change.
